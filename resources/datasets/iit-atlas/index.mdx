name: IIT Atlas
description: >-
  The IIT Human Brain Atlas (v.5.0) contains anatomical, DTI, HARDI templates,
  probabilistic gray matter labels, probabilistic connectivity-based white
  matter labels, and major fiber-bundles of the adult human brain in ICBM-152
  space. Artifact-free MRI data from 72 subjects were used in the development of
  the atlas.
